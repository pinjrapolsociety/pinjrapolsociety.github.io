About the NGO

Established in 1939, the Pinjra Pol Society has been providing service to the holy cows in the Saharanpur district.

Services we provide

Services we provide at the Gaushala

Maintainence of Gaushala

Maintaing cleanliness of the Gaushala.

Maintaining water supply for cleaning and drinking.

Maintaining lights, fans and exhaust fans in the cow barracks.

Maintaing vessels of chara and water in the cow barracks.

Health and hygine of the cows

Arranging doctor for health check-ups and treatment of cows.

Providing timely feed to the cows.

Bathing the cows to keep them clean.

Managing the finances of Gaushala

Purchasing of the cow feed (chara).

Salaries of the Gausewaks.

Managing the financial accounts.

Managing the accounts of milk distribution.

Planning towards self-sustainability

Building of bathing showers in cow barracks.

Preparing medicines from cow urine.

Making mosquito coils from cow dung.

Creation of a fertility centre.
